Questions

Before you start.

Is the screening really free?

Yes. The screening and specialist follow-up are free. No card, no account.

Does this replace my vet?

Never. Diagnosis and treatment belong with the vet who knows your cat. We help you show up prepared, with the right questions, not a second opinion from the internet.

My cat seems totally healthy. Why screen?

That's the best time. Cats hide illness. The same five questions work at any age. They catch early changes before they become emergencies.

Will a feline specialist call me?

Only if your result suggests follow-up. Then a feline specialist calls within 24 hours to explain what it means and what to ask your vet. Low-risk results don't need a call.

Are the questions different for kittens vs seniors?

No. Every cat gets the same five questions: water, urination, weight, appetite, and vomiting. Age helps us interpret the result, not change the check.

What happens after my specialist call?

They explain what your result means in plain language and what to ask your vet. Diagnosis and treatment always stay with the vet who knows your cat.

Can kittens use this?

Yes. The same five questions work at any age. For kittens, we watch for growth, appetite, and digestion patterns that can signal problems early.

Can I use it every month?

Absolutely. Many parents re-screen when habits shift — more water, litter box changes, or appetite dips. It's free every time.

How accurate is it?

It's a screening tool, not a diagnosis. It flags patterns feline specialists watch for — so you know when to monitor and when to see your vet sooner.
